How to make French Toast

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Directions:

  1. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  2. Melt the butter in a large non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides evenly.
  4. Place the bread in the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Repeat with the remaining bread slices, adding more butter to the skillet as needed.

That's it! French Toast is a classic breakfast dish that's quick and easy to make, and can be customized to suit your tastes. Enjoy!

How to make Italian Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz. of pasta (such as spaghetti, fettuccine, or penne)
  • 1-2 cloves of garlic(minced)
  • 2-3 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package directions, until al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and saut√© for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  4. Drain the cooked pasta, reserving a cup of the pasta water.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the garlic and toss to combine, adding some of the reserved pasta water as needed to loosen the sauce.
  6. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta and toss to combine.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.

That's it! Italian pasta is a simple yet flavorful dish that can be made with just a few ingredients. You can also add other ingredients to the sauce, such as cherry tomatoes, olives, or mushrooms, to give it more flavor and texture. Enjoy!
